About Us

Welcome to Donut Delights, where every ring of dough is a little taste of happiness! We are dedicated to serving you the most delightful, mouthwatering donuts that will leave you craving for another bite. we believe that every donut should be a work of art and meticulously crafted with the finest ingredients and a touch of love.




Our Story

Founded in 2012 by donut aficionado Sawant, Donut Point started as a humble food truck with a simple goal, to make the world a sweeter place, one donut at a time. His passion for crafting the perfect donut led him to experiment with flavors and techniques until he had created the ultimate donut experience.
